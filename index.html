FASHION MADE EASY

B.A.T Wurld is a skill development hub for fashion designing.

We offer after school fashion club, home lessons and online fashion courses for kids, teens and adults.

B.A.T Wurld fashion house is the best centre to intro basic and advance sewing concept, embroiding, making designs on materials, printing, cresting on clothes and consultation on your fashion sense.

Interestingly, these skills are foundational as it helps children, teens and adult to embrace fashion as a way you reach out to people in our environment as they say the way you dress is the way you will be address.
